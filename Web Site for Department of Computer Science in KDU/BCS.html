BSc (Hons) in Computer Science

Overview

  • The BSc (Hons) in Computer Science program offers a comprehensive education in computer science and related fields. Students will gain hands-on experience and knowledge in various areas of computer science.

Program Highlights

  • Duration: 4 years

    Focus areas: Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and more.

    Practical projects and internships

    Experienced faculty

    State-of-the-art facilities

    Pass the Interview
